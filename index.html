The World Through Our Eyes

TWTOExpo is a visual arts exhibition designed from a group of high students (2019), seeking to create a medium of artistic expression for all high scholars in Albania and maybe beyond in a near future. This exhibition welcomes all work that is important to students because we have faith that the time one dedicates to creation is what makes creation powerful and important. Thus, we welcome any high scholar to send us their paintings, drawings, photographs, videos and any other visual medium they may have worked on, for we value all they value to take the time to work on. Anyone who creates deserves to be able to share their creations, what they believe it’s most important for the world’s eye to see. This activity, together with our partners CLMB & OFICINA aim to promote young artists to further competitions and possible internship or educational experience.

How to participate?

TWTOExpo aims to give life and promote the way youth students see the world. Everyone is free to apply with a maximum of 10 art works. We do not want to limit your creativity, so everyone can apply with the most beautiful pictures they poses.

The work you apply with should be yours and authentic.

How we started?

"As everything else, TWTOExpo started just as any other idea. I had just reopened the photography club in my school, and we were attending the presentation of the Multimedia TAF contest, where high scholars should create a 7 min video about a given topic. And i was surprised by the number of students that actually were there and wanted to take part and express them self's. So i thought why not create an event where all this young people can express them self's and their work. So that day i talked with my friends from other high schools and even tho most of the schools didn't have a well organised photography club there were people who actually wanted to take part. So this is how an IDEA got to evolve in some actual work"

- Mario Alla
